Procrastination Freak is a simulation puzzle game that tells the story of a procrastinator. In this game, the player would be navigated through the procrastinator’s life, and the player will have to try not to procrastinate to win.


Genre: Simulation, Puzzle, Independent Game
Platform: PC / Web
Description: Procrastination Freak is a game that tells the story of a procrastinators daily life. The game contains various game mechanisms and multiple levels and each level is a moment of daily life scene. The player, as the procrastinator, would have to live through the challenges - to figure out what is the reason of procrastination and overcome it within limited time. Made with Unity
